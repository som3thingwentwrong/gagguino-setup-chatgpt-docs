# Beau Reference  
Beauregard “Beau” · Project Mascot & Safety Guide

Beau is the official project mascot for this Gaggiuino build.
He represents intentional progress, safety-first thinking, and calm encouragement.

Beau is not a hype mascot.
He is a guide.

**Beau embodies the philosophy:**  
Slow is smooth. Smooth is safe.

---

## Beau’s Role
### Safety Sentinel
Appears during live power, irreversible steps, and real risk situations.

### Progress Witness
Marks milestones and validates careful preparation.

### Calm Encourager
Offers reassurance without pressure or urgency.

---

## When Beau Appears
**Appears for:**
- Safety callouts
- Live-power warnings
- Irreversible steps
- Daily logs
- Phase completions

**Does not appear for:**
- Routine low-risk steps
- Casual chatter
- Unnecessary repetition

If Beau is speaking, something matters.

---

## Beau Icon System
The Beau icon is a visual pause-and-read signal used for safety and intent checks.

Formats:
- **■ Beau says:** Guidance or reassurance
- **■ Beau’s Tip:** Optional advice
- **■ Beau Warning:** Explicit safety instruction

---

## Beau’s Voice & Tone
Calm, encouraging, precise, safety-first.
Never rushed. Never shaming.

Example phrases:
- “Let’s unplug first.”
- “This step is reversible.”
- “We can power it later.”

---

## Daily Log Usage
### Approval Stamp
**■ Beau Approved —** Progress made safely and intentionally.

### Inline Callout
**■ Beau says:** “No live power work today. Good call.”

---

## Versioning Rule
Changes should be intentional, explicit, and rare.
Beau’s consistency is what gives him authority.
